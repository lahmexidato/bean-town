Mexico

Café de olla

+ pan dulce

Cinnamon, piloncillo, and a clay pot. Made slowly, drunk slowly, usually before anything else happens in the day. This is the one your abuela had a strong opinion about.

Ethiopia

Jebena ceremony

+ roasted barley

Coffee was born here. The jebena ceremony — three rounds, same grounds, each cup lighter than the last — might be the most important thing anyone has ever done with a coffee plant.

Japan

Kissaten drip

+ wagashi

Japan took coffee, slowed it down, and built a whole room around it. The kissaten — a serious coffee shop, not a café — is one of the quietest, most considered things you can walk into.
