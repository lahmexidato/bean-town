voices worth following

A short list. Not comprehensive, just the things we'd send someone who said "I want to actually understand coffee" without wanting a twelve-part masterclass.

YouTube, free

James Hoffmann

The best explainer in coffee. Hoffmann has the rare ability to be technically precise without being condescending. His videos on espresso, filter, and origin are the closest thing to a coffee education that doesn't require a barista certification. Start with his "What is Specialty Coffee?" video.

youtube.com/@jameshoffmann →

Book

The World Atlas of Coffee

Also by Hoffmann. Country by country, origin by origin, the physical object of understanding coffee as a global crop. Not a technical manual; more like a well-researched travel guide for the curious drinker. The photography alone makes it worth owning.

James Hoffmann, 2014 (updated 2018)
